I am a bioinformatics research programmer at the Genome Science Center in Vancouver, BC, affiliated with BC Cancer Agency and currently working in the Steif lab. My research focuses on cancer genomics, particularly in the analysis of long-read and single-cell sequencing data. I am also very interested in programming, self learning python, machine learning and web development.

I earned a Master's of Science in Genome Science and Technology from the University of British Columbia in 2023, where I honed my skills in cancer genomics, bioinformatics, and data analysis. Prior to that, I graduated from the University of Toronto in 2021 with a degree in genomics and molecular biology (with a minor in psychology), which sparked my passion for genetics.
